Your final assignment:

Test what you've learned for yourself! Here are our recommendations for what you can do master the "palms up" method of persuasion:

Grab a video camera (or a mirror).

Practice saying one sentence that is meant to be persuasive (like, "I am the best candidate for this presidency and you should definitely vote for me!") using the palms-up method. Note what kinds of gestures look and feel more natural to you and practice those in your daily life.

Grab a friend.

Practice the palms-up method with a friend, using persuasive sentences that are more relevant to you (i.e., if you work in pharmaceuticals, you could practice "selling" one of your products to your friend). Have them give you feedback on what they think works best.

Test it out in real life.

Try convincing two or three (unknowing!) people of the same thing using the different gestures. See which one works out the best!
